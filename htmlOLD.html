Welcome to Numbers, a version of the popular game Higher or Lower. The aim of the game is to guess the randomly selected integer between two given integers.

You do this by entering an integer and the computer answers with “higher” if the integer you entered is higher than the randomized integer or “lower” if the given integer is lower than the randomized integer.

When you start the game, you get a choice of how many and which bots you want to battle against. As well as a short description about how each of the bots work.
